- Welcome -

Welcome to my retro-computing and emulation site! This website was put together as a project for the web-development course offered by the DEVELOPER Academy but is also a nice way to showcase my interest in retro computing / gaming and the emulation of consoles and computers from the past few decades.

The various sections of this site can be accessed via the navbar above, or the Site Menu to the left on tablet and larger devices. A number of external resources which you may find interesting or useful are also provided below or to the right, including - on this page - some links to resources which I actually used to create the site.
